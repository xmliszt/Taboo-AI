## Diverse collection of topics at your fingertips

Finding your preferred topics has never been easier. **Sort by various criteria**, and see which topics are **most popular**. Discover what fellow players are diving into! 📚

Taboo AI has an ever-growing list of topics, ranging from everyday life to advanced academic subjects. Curated by us and AI, these topics are designed to help you learn English in a fun and engaging way.

Moreover, Taboo AI welcomes new topics contributions from our thriving community. If you have a topic in mind, feel free to suggest it to us! 🌟

If you still can't find the topic you are looking for, try out [AI generated topics](/ai)!

## Conversational gameplay, fun and engaging

![3](https://github.com/xmliszt/resources/blob/main/taboo-ai/images/v300/v300-3.jpg?raw=true)

Taboo AI's gameplay is designed to be **fun and engaging**. With a **simple and intuitive interface**, you can easily navigate through the game. You will be provided with a target word, and an intelligent conversation will be initiated between you and the AI. Your goal is to provide clues to help the AI guess the target word, but there's a catch - you can't use the word itself, or any of the `taboo` words listed below it. 🤐

## Comprehensive results and ranking system

![4](https://github.com/xmliszt/resources/blob/main/taboo-ai/images/v300/v300-4.jpg?raw=true)

Taboo AI offers **comprehensive results** after each game. You can see how well you did, and which clues were the most effective. Our intelligent AI will provide feedback on your clues, and suggest ways to improve them. Even better, our AI can provide example clues from which you can learn correct English grammar and better word choices. 📊

In topics selection, you can turn on "ranking mode" to see how your score compares to other players, and how you rank in the community. 🏆

## Beautiful sharing card for your results

![3](https://github.com/xmliszt/resources/blob/main/taboo-ai/images/v300/v3010-0.jpg?raw=true)

Want to show off your English talents? You can easily share your amazing results with your friends and the community at the results page. Your clues are scored and rated and Taboo AI will generate a beautiful sharing card for your convenience!

## Both dark and light mode available

Tailor your gaming environment to suit your style. Whether you prefer a **dark** or **light** backdrop, Taboo AI now supports both modes for your convenience.
